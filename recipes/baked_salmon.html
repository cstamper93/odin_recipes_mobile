Succulent Salmon with Roasted Sweet Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 2 8 oz wild caught salmon
  • 2 sweet potatoes
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 tsp salt
  • 4 tsp pepper
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp rosemary
  • 2 tsp thyme

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Peel and cut potatoes into 1 in cubes.
  3. On a baking sheet with aluminum foil, place salmon and sweet potatoes on separate halves.
  4. Coat salmon and potatoes with olive oil. Season both salmons with 1 tbsp of salt and pepper, and half of a tsp of other herbs. Use the remaining spices and herbs for the potatoes.
  5. When oven is preheated, put baking sheet in oven for 20 minutes or until desired doneness is reached.
