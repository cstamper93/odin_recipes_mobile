Eggs and Toast

Tasty Fluffy Eggs with Your Favorite Toasted Bread

This recipe is for a good ol' reliable breakfast! However, it can be enjoyed any time of the day. Use any type of bread that you enjoy the most. I use rye for the extra flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 slice of bread
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • (optional) 2 tsp chopped parsley
  • (optional) 1 pat butter

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and stir to scramble
  2. In a skillet, add olive oil on medium heat
  3. Pour eggs into skillet with olive oil. Let them cook until edges start getting firm.
  4. Add salt and pepper to eggs
  5. While eggs begin to cook, begin toasting your eggs to desired crispiness
  6. Start scraping under the edges of the eggs with a spatula. Flip when firm enough to stay intact.
  7. Turn off heat and allow eggs to cook until desired firmness is reached
  8. Move your eggs and toast onto plate. Garnish your eggs with parsley and butter your toast if you desire, and enjoy!
