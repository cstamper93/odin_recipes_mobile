Tex Mex Chicken Bowl

Flavorful Tex Mex Chicken Bowl

Many flavors collide to create an authentic Mexican eating experience!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken breast
  • Taco Bell chicken seasoning mix
  • Chicken broth
  • 1 cup Jasmine rice
  • 1 8 oz can black beans
  • 1 red onion, chopped
  • 2 roma tomatoes, diced
  • 4 limes
  • 1 bunch cilantro
  • 2 cups shredded cheese

Steps

  1. Remove any unwanted fat from chicken. Place in crockpot with 1-2 cups of chicken broth. Turn crock pot on high and cook for 3 hours.
  2. 30 minutes before chicken is done, prepare rice, chop cilantro, dice tomato, cut limes in half, and heat black beans.
  3. Bring 1.5 cups water to a boil. Add rice, cover, and reduce to a simmer. Let cook for 15 min.
  4. Combine ingredients, and squeeze half of 1 lime on top. Enjoy!
